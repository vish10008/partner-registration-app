FAQ'S

Q. How do I get the order number?

A. When you place an online order via partner app/compose OR place it with our sales rep, an automated email is generated with the order number & details. Check your mail inbox OR partner app or compose.

Q. How to make a bulk payment for multiple orders?

A. The easiest way to make a bulk payment is via partner app & website. Please try now. In case you’re doing it via other modes, please provide the order number (this can be any of the order number that you are paying for) or Mobile No. (if you don’t have the order no.)
